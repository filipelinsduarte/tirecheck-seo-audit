Technical SEO · Critical Findings

Four Issues Blocking Organic Growth

  • Zero organic keyword rankings domain-wideAhrefs reports 0 rankings for tirecheck.online. Not a data gap - Google assigns no positions to any page. Root cause must be diagnosed via Search Console URL inspection immediately.
  • Hreflang tags missing across all three language versionsEN, DE, and TR variants exist (Polylang) but hreflang is absent. Google may treat all three as duplicate content, suppressing international rankings entirely.
  • No structured data on any product or blog pageZero schema markup - no Product, FAQ, or Article schema. The site is ineligible for rich results across every SERP feature Google offers.
  • Help centre isolated on a separate subdomainhelp.tirecheck.online builds authority independently. Migrating to tirecheck.online/help/ consolidates all backlinks and link equity into the main domain.
